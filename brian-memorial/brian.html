Brian Malmon's wit and booming bass will live on in the minds and hearts of Uptown Vocalians forever. We tragically lost "Buzz" between his seventh and eighth semesters in the group, after his serving faithfully as President and Overseer of Bass Resonance in 1999. He passed away on March 24, 2000 at his home in Potomac, MD.

This page is a space for members and others who knew or performed with Buzz to remember him through essays, poetry, and the like. If you would like to contribute, please mail David at dke4@columbia.edu.

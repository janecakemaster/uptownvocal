WELCOME.

Uptown Vocal is the premier jazz and pop a cappella group of Columbia University in the city of New York. As we enter our third decade of bringing jazz/pop to the CU community, our repertoire includes major pop hits like Beyonce Knowles' "Single Ladies" and Ben Folds' "Still Fighting It", as well as timeless jazz standards such as "My Funny Valentine" and "Fly Me to the Moon". We perform at numerous functions both on and off campus throughout the year and regularly grace other college campuses with our musical talent and unusual attractiveness.

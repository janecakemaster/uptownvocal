Alumni

Uptown Vocal was founded in 1991 by Rachel Tepfer and Rachel Harmon (née Rezak), but back then we weren't known as Uptown Vocal. In fact we didn't really have a name, we were just a close group of friends united in their love of singing, groovin' and creating tight vocal harmonies. It wasn't until 1995, after having tried out a few names like the "CU-Rhytmics" and "The CATatonics" (hence our love of cats), that we finally put it to a vote and declared ourselves as the one and only Uptown Vocal! We didn't start out as a jazz group; that's something we picked up along the way.
